# Design System

## Visual Tone
- Modern editorial
- Calm, warm, professional
- Premium but restrained

## Typography
Headings:
- Axiforma
- H1: font-semibold (600)
- H2/H3: font-medium (500)

Body:
- Sans-serif
- font-normal (400)
- leading-relaxed

UI labels:
- font-medium (500)

## Colors
- Background: warm off-white
- Text: charcoal (not pure black)
- Accent: warm orange-coral (used sparingly)

## Corners
- Cards: rounded-2xl
- Buttons: rounded-xl
- Inputs: rounded-lg

## Motion
- Subtle fades and transitions
- No playful animations
